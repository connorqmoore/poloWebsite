CMU Water Polo

Team

CMU Water Polo is a Club sport at Carnegie Mellon University that competes at the NCAA divison 3 club level. Our team is comprised of players from all different background. We pride ourselves on our diversity, but our number one concentration is, and will continue to be competing at a national level. During the 2013 season we competed at Franklin Marshall, and this season we have qualified to participate at the national competition at Bowdin Maine.
